Need Help?

Welcome to the City Council Events Portal help page. This guide will walk you through using the portal.

Navigation

  • Use the top menu to jump to Home, Events, or Contact sections.
  • Click "Help" to open this page in a new tab.

Registering for an Event

  • Go to the "Events" section.
  • Fill in your name, email, event type, and other required fields.
  • Click "Submit" to register. A confirmation message will appear.

Finding Nearby Events

  • Click the "Find Nearby Events" button.
  • Allow location access when prompted by your browser.
  • Your location will appear below the button.

  • Scroll down to the video section.

Saving and Clearing Preferences

  • Your selected event type is saved automatically.
  • Click "Clear Preferences" to remove saved choices.
